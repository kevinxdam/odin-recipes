Healthy Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is a popular and versatile dish that features a creamy and rich mashed avocado spread on toasted bread. It combines the smoothness of ripe avocados with a hint of tanginess from lemon juice and the perfect balance of salt and pepper. The creamy avocado mixture is generously slathered onto crispy toasted bread slices, creating a delightful contrast of textures. Optional toppings like red pepper flakes, sliced tomatoes, or a poached egg can be added for an extra burst of flavor and variety. Avocado toast is not only delicious and satisfying but also offers a nutritious and wholesome meal or snack, making it a beloved choice for breakfast, brunch, or any time of the day.

Ingredients:
  • Ripe avocados
  • Bread slices (toasted)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: red pepper flakes, sliced tomatoes, poached eggs, etc.
Instructions:
  1. Cut open the avocados and remove the pits. Scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Squeeze a splash of lemon juice over the mashed avocado to prevent browning and add a hint of tanginess.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well to combine.
  5. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto toasted bread slices.
  6. Optionally, add your preferred toppings such as red pepper flakes for some heat, sliced tomatoes for freshness, or a poached egg for added protein.
  7. Serve immediately and savor the creamy and flavorful goodness of avocado toast.
