Organic and Healthy Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is a classic and refreshing Italian dish that showcases the vibrant flavors of fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves. The salad is beautifully arranged with alternating tomato and mozzarella slices, with basil leaves tucked in between. A drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar adds a delightful tang and enhances the flavors. With a sprinkle of salt and pepper, the Caprese Salad becomes a harmonious combination of textures and tastes. It's a light and flavorful salad that celebrates the simplicity and quality of its ingredients. Perfect for summer or any time of the year, Caprese Salad is a delightful appetizer or side dish that captures the essence of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:
  • Fresh tomatoes (sliced)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Arrange the tomato slices and mozzarella cheese slices on a serving platter, alternating between them.
  2. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
  4. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over the salad, adjusting the amount to your taste.
  5. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the salad to season.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the vibrant flavors of the Caprese Salad.
